Dr. Perumal M

Founder

Dr. Perumal M is the founder of Dhanya skin and hair clinic. He has done his MD in Dermatology at the prestigious Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education & Research (JIPMER) PONDICHERRY. He also has worked as a senior resident in JIPMER for around 3 years. Working in the apex institute has provided him vast experience and knowledge in every field of Dermatology. Whether it is pediatric dermatology or cosmetic surgeries or clinical dermatology, Dr. Perumal has proven himself as a hair and skin expert. During his tenure he has gained vast experience in managing difficult dermatology issues, Dermatosurgery (acne scar surgery, vitiligo surgery, mole removal, scar revision, ear lobe repair, nail surgery) and Cosmetology (Microdermabrasion, Chemical peeling, Microneedling)
